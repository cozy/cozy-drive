Cozy.io is a platform that brings all your web services in the same private space. With it, your web apps and your devices can share data easily, providing you with a new experience. You can install Cozy on your own hardware where no one profiles you.

This is the iOS mobile client to your personnal Cozy server. Connect it to your Cozy server and browse through your cozy files from your iOS device.

Use github issues to report bugs and vote for next features : https://github.com/cozy/cozy-mobile/issues
